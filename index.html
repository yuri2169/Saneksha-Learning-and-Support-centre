Saneksha was built on a quiet, stubborn belief: that every child is already capable. They just need someone patient enough to find the door they learn through — and brave enough to walk in with them.

We are a specialised remediation centre for children with Specific Learning Difficulties (SLD) and ADHD, ages 5 to 14. Our work is structured, evidence-based, and deeply personal. Each child is assessed individually, met without judgement, and supported with a plan made only for them.

Our approach

Three quiet steps. One growing child.

Progress at Saneksha isn't dramatic. It's small, honest, repeated. A page read without flinching. A sum finished without a meltdown. A child who suddenly says, "I can do this." That's the work.

i.

Understand

We begin with listening. Through a thorough assessment and unhurried conversations with you, we learn your child's strengths, struggles, interests, and the way their mind likes to move.

ii.

Support

We build a plan that is structured but never rigid — gentle, evidence-based, and shaped around the child in front of us. Therapies and learning are integrated, not stacked.

iii.

Grow

We focus on small, steady progress. We celebrate it loudly. And we share it with you regularly so the home becomes part of the classroom and the classroom part of the home.

A note for parents

If you are reading this tired, we see you.

You may have spent years explaining your child to teachers, doctors, and family members. You may have been told to be patient, or to be firm, or to wait it out. You may be exhausted from advocating for someone who can't yet advocate for themselves.

We won't pretend to know your child yet. But we know how to listen carefully, speak honestly, and build something real with you. Reach out — even just to talk. There's no cost, no commitment, and no judgement on the other end of the line.
